Introducing Opinion Wars


Knowing the overall public opinion/sentiment on a particular topic is an important factor to be considered while making many important descisions (such as weather a person should buy/sell a given asset). And it is important that we determine the overall public sentiment on things accurately. The current system (like twitter polls, like/dislike ratios, etc.) work on the principle that all votes carry equal weightage. This approach often misrepresents the true public sentiment as it doesnt take into consideration how strong each voters opinion is. For example assume that there is a twitter poll, where people could vote 'yes' or 'no' on weather they believe in price of dogecoin will higher within a year from now. And assume that 60% people believe that the price of doge will be lower and say that they are willing to short the market with a cumulative ammount of say X dollars. Now it is very possible that the 40% of people who agree have a very strong opinion on doge and is willing to long with a cumulative amount of 3X dollars. Here the overall market sentiment is positive and the price is likely to go higher even though 60% people disagree because the people who voted 'yes' agree more strongly than the people who voted 'no'. Therefore, taking a poll and making all the votes count the same isnt the best approach for knowing the true overall sentiment. For some topics it is extremely important to factor in how strong a person agrees/disagrees. And we believe that the best way to determine how strong a persons opinion is to see how much they are willing to risk for that opinion. Opnion wars is a decentralized protocol where use a better approach to derive the true public sentiment on various topics in a way where people can put their money where their mouth is and possibly win a lot of crypto along the way.
